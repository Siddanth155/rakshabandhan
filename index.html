Dear Sister,

As we celebrate Raksha Bandhan today, I find myself reflecting on the incredible bond we've shared throughout our lives. From the little moments of laughter to the times of profound understanding, you have been a guiding light in my life, illuminating my path with your wisdom, kindness, and love. You have not only been my sister but also my protector, my teacher, and my most trusted friend. Your presence has been a source of comfort, and your support has given me the courage to face life’s challenges. In your eyes, I've always found encouragement, and in your heart, I've always known I am loved. Your strength, compassion, and grace inspire me, and I am so proud to call you my sister. No matter where life takes us, the bond we share will remain unbreakable. I am eternally grateful to have you in my life, and I promise to cherish and honor our relationship forever.
Happy Raksha Bandhan, my dear sister. 😁

🌼With all my love and gratitude,🌼
